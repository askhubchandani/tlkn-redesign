tlkn Redesign

My take on UI/UX of tlkn App.

tlkn is one of the beautifully designed voice calling apps, which doesn't support text messages or emojis just because it wants users to have real conversations.
Even though this app is pretty to look at, it does have some UI/UX problems that are discussed below.

Faults/New Suggestions:
1) Unable to view the top bar (date,time,network), because of entire app being white.
2) Contacts should have an alphabetical scroll bar.
3) Add money feature should have text box along with scroll bar.
4) Final add money is showing only Euro, even when the currency is changed.
5) Dialler should also work as search bar.
